## SECTION: Problem Statement

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Heading
The Freight Pricing Challenge

Freight pricing is one of the most complex, time-sensitive operations in logistics — and still one of the most manual.

Every freight quote is a race against the clock. Customers expect rates in minutes. Carriers update pricing daily. Routes shift with seasonality, capacity, and fuel. Yet most logistics providers still build quotations through spreadsheets, email chains, and disconnected carrier portals — losing deals to faster competitors and leaving margin on the table with every shipment.

### Common operational pain points
- Manual freight rate calculations across multiple carriers and modes
- Delayed customer quote responses — hours or days, not minutes
- Inconsistent pricing across carriers, regions, and sales teams
- Rapidly changing market rates that spreadsheets can't keep up with
- Lack of centralised pricing intelligence across the business
- Difficulty optimising margins across high-volume shipments
- Limited visibility into historical pricing trends and carrier performance

## SECTION: Platform Architecture — How FreightIQ Works

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

### Section subheading
A 5-stage pricing intelligence stack — from carrier connectivity to customer-ready quote, in seconds.

### Stages

| Stage | Phase                          | What Happens |
|-------|--------------------------------|--------------|
| 01    | Carrier Connectivity           | Real-time API integrations pull live rates, capacity, and availability from multiple carriers across modes (LCL, FCL, air, multi-modal). |
| 02    | Pricing Intelligence Layer     | AI engine processes carrier inputs against demand signals, route conditions, urgency, and seasonality to generate market-aware pricing. |
| 03    | Margin & Optimisation Engine   | Pricing options are evaluated for cost, delivery speed, carrier competitiveness, and margin impact — surfacing the best-fit options. |
| 04    | Historical Analytics Engine    | Continuously learning layer uses historical shipment, rate, and margin data to refine pricing recommendations and surface trends. |
| 05    | Quote Generation & Delivery    | Customer-ready freight quotations are generated automatically and delivered to sales teams or directly to customers via integrations. |

### Closing line
FreightIQ doesn't just calculate freight rates — it optimises every quote for speed, margin, and competitiveness.
