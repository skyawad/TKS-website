## SECTION: Problem Statement

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Heading
The Enterprise Document Challenge

Enterprises process millions of documents daily — invoices, claims, contracts, shipping papers, KYC files, handwritten forms. Yet despite digital transformation, most document processing is still manual, error-prone, and slow. Traditional OCR tools handle text but miss context. Hand-written documents break template-based OCR. Multi-layout PDFs fragment across pages.

### Common operational pain points
- Manual data entry and validation across business processes
- Slow turnaround and operational bottlenecks
- High error rates in document handling and re-keying
- Complex PDFs and tables that defeat template-based OCR
- Inconsistent, unstructured document formats
- Time-consuming compliance, KYC, and verification workflows
- Limited scalability of back-office and shared-service teams

### Closing impact
The result: rising operational costs, compliance exposure, slower customer experience, and friction for both teams and end-users.

## SECTION: Platform Architecture — How ParseIQ Works

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

### Section subheading
A layered AI architecture that turns any document into structured business data.

ParseIQ is built on a multi-layer AI engine — combining traditional OCR, computer vision, AI classification, and LLM reasoning — so it can act on a virtually any document type or format.

### Layers (5)

| Layer | Stage                                  | What Happens |
|-------|----------------------------------------|--------------|
| 01    | Ingestion                              | Documents come in via API, watch-folder, email, or batch upload — PDFs, images, scans, handwritten forms. |
| 02    | Smart OCR & Pre-Processing             | Image enhancement, deskewing, and intelligent OCR extract raw text — including handwritten content. |
| 03    | AI Classification & Layout Understanding | AI models identify document type, layout, and key regions; classification routes the document to the right extraction logic. |
| 04    | LLM-Driven Contextual Extraction       | LLMs interpret context, extract complex fields, validate values, and reason across multi-page documents. |
| 05    | Structured Output & Delivery           | Clean JSON, CSV, or API payloads are delivered downstream — ready for ERP, CRM, KYC, or workflow consumption. |

### Closing line
The result is a platform that doesn't just read documents — it understands them.

## SECTION: Use Cases

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Use-case cards (5)

#### 1. KYC & Identity Verification Automation
Onboard customers faster with AI-powered identity extraction. ParseIQ extracts and validates passport, national ID, and proof-of-address documents using MRZ parsing and AI validation. Banks, fintechs, and digital wallets reduce KYC turnaround from hours to seconds while improving compliance.
*Document types:* Passports · National IDs · Utility Bills · Proof of Address.
*Outcome:* Faster onboarding · Lower drop-off · Audit-ready KYC.

#### 2. Claims & Policy Document Processing
Automate insurance claims intake end-to-end. Insurers use ParseIQ to extract structured data from claim forms, medical bills, repair estimates, and policy documents. Adjusters get clean, validated inputs instantly — accelerating claim cycles and reducing leakage.
*Document types:* Claim Forms · Policy Docs · Medical Bills · Repair Estimates.
*Outcome:* Faster claims cycle · Lower leakage · Higher CSAT.

#### 3. Invoice & Vendor Document Automation
Touchless AP processing across logistics and operations. Extract line items, totals, GST/VAT, and vendor details from invoices, PODs, and shipping documents — even with varying formats. ParseIQ pushes structured records directly into ERP and AP workflows for touchless invoicing.
*Document types:* Invoices · PODs · Bills of Lading · Vendor Contracts.
*Outcome:* Touchless AP · Faster vendor payments · Reduced exceptions.

#### 4. Medical Records & Healthcare Forms Digitisation
Convert paper-based patient data into structured records. ParseIQ extracts data from handwritten prescriptions, insurance documents, lab reports, and insurance authorisations. Hospitals and payers digitise patient records into EHR-ready formats while preserving accuracy and compliance-aligned formats.
*Document types:* Patient Forms · Prescriptions · Lab Reports · Insurance Authorisations.
*Outcome:* EHR-ready data · Faster admissions · Compliance-aligned.

#### 5. Government & Citizen Document Processing
Automate citizen-facing application and verification workflows. Public-sector agencies use ParseIQ to process citizen applications, AI-driven extraction and validation accelerate case handling, and reduce backlog across high-volume citizen services.
*Document types:* Passport Applications · Citizen IDs · Government Forms · Public Records.
*Outcome:* Reduced backlog · Faster case handling · Better citizen experience.
