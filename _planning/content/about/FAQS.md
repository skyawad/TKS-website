## SECTION: Engagement & Services FAQs (10)

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

#### 1. How can I contact Techknomatic?
You can reach our team through the Contact Us form on the website, email us directly, or connect with our regional offices in the UAE, Oman, and India for business inquiries, partnerships, and support.

#### 2. How quickly can I expect a response?
Our team usually responds within **one business day, Sun–Thu (GST, UTC+4)** for all inquiries related to services, partnerships, demos, and support requests. WhatsApp is typically faster.

#### 3. Can I request a demo of your solutions?
Yes. You can request a personalised demo for our Business Analytics, AI, Data Engineering, Geospatial Analytics, and automation platforms — including AssistIQ, TicketIQ, CallOps AI, CXO Nexus, InsightSM, DataPulseIQ, ParseIQ, and FreightIQ.

#### 4. Do you provide consultation sessions before starting a project?
Absolutely. We offer initial discovery and consultation sessions to understand your business challenges, existing systems, and analytics requirements — at no charge before scoping.

#### 5. Can Techknomatic support projects outside the UAE and India?
Yes. We work with clients across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, Oman, India, the USA, and other international markets — with UAE-resident senior staff leading Gulf engagements.

#### 6. What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with startups, enterprises, government organisations, and large corporations across industries such as Insurance, Oil & Gas, Banking, Manufacturing, Logistics, Retail, ITSM, and Utilities.

#### 7. Can I contact Techknomatic for partnership opportunities?
Yes. We welcome collaboration opportunities with technology partners, OEMs, system integrators, and consulting firms. See `/about/partnerships` for our current alliance model.

#### 8. Do you offer support for existing analytics platforms?
Yes. We provide support, optimisation, migration, and enhancement services for existing BI, Data Engineering, and AI environments — including Power BI, Tableau, Qlik, ServiceNow, BMC Remedy, and Jira SM.

#### 9. Can I request onsite meetings or workshops?
Yes. Our team can arrange onsite meetings, workshops, capability presentations, and solution walkthroughs in Dubai, Riyadh, Muscat, and other locations based on project requirements.

#### 10. Do you provide Proof of Concept (POC) services?
Yes. We can help organisations validate use cases through pilot implementations and Proof of Concepts before full-scale deployment.

## SECTION: Career FAQs (15)

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

#### 1. Why should I join Techknomatic Careers?
At Techknomatic, you get the opportunity to work on real-world Data Analytics, AI, Business Intelligence, and Digital Transformation projects across multiple industries and global markets. We focus on innovation, ownership, learning, and career growth.

#### 2. What kind of roles does Techknomatic hire for?
We hire across:
- Data Engineering
- Business Intelligence & Visualisation
- AI & Machine Learning
- Geospatial Analytics
- Cloud & Data Platforms
- Full-Stack Development
- QA & Automation
- Sales & Business Development
- Project Management
- Marketing & Operations

#### 3. Do you hire freshers?
Yes. We actively hire freshers and early-career professionals who are passionate about analytics, technology, and problem-solving.

#### 4. What technologies can I work on at Techknomatic?
You may get opportunities to work on:
- Qlik
- Microsoft Power BI
- Esri
- SQL & Cloud Platforms (Azure / AWS / GCP)
- Python & AI frameworks
- Modern Data Engineering tools (Snowflake, Databricks, Talend, Airflow)
- Automation & Integration platforms

#### 5. Does Techknomatic provide training and certifications?
Yes. We encourage continuous learning and support employees with training programs, certifications, mentorship, and hands-on project exposure.

#### 6. What is the work culture like at Techknomatic?
We promote a collaborative, growth-oriented, and innovation-driven culture where employees are encouraged to take ownership, share ideas, and grow professionally.

#### 7. Are there opportunities to work on international projects?
Yes. Our teams work on projects across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman, India, the USA, and other global markets.

#### 8. Does Techknomatic offer hybrid or remote work options?
Depending on the role and project requirements, we offer flexible work models including onsite, hybrid, and remote opportunities.

#### 9. What is the hiring process at Techknomatic?
Resume Screening → Initial Discussion → Technical / Functional Assessment → Interview Rounds → Final HR Discussion → Offer.

#### 10. What kind of growth opportunities can I expect?
- Work on enterprise-scale projects
- Learn emerging technologies
- Move across domains and technologies
- Take leadership responsibilities
- Build client-facing expertise

#### 11. Do you offer internship opportunities?
Yes. We offer internship opportunities for students and graduates interested in Data Analytics, AI, BI, Software Development, and related domains.

#### 12. How can I apply for a job at Techknomatic?
You can apply through the Careers page by submitting your profile or sending your resume to **careers@techknomatic.com**.

#### 13. What qualities do you look for in candidates?
Problem-solving mindset · Willingness to learn · Ownership & accountability · Communication skills · Technical curiosity · Team collaboration.

#### 14. Will I get exposure to clients and real business problems?
Yes. Our teams work closely with customers to solve real business challenges, giving employees valuable industry exposure and practical learning.

#### 15. Does Techknomatic support career progression internally?
Absolutely. We believe in growing talent internally and provide opportunities for role expansion, leadership growth, and cross-functional exposure.
