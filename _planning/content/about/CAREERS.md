## SECTION: Our Hiring Process

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

### Steps (5)

2. **Initial Discussion** — a brief conversation to understand your goals and current trajectory.
3. **Technical / Case Round** — showcase your problem-solving abilities and relevant expertise.
4. **Final Fitment Discussion** — meet the leadership team to discuss values and long-term vision.
5. **Offer** — welcome to the team where your potential for growth is prime.
