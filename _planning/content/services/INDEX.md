## SECTION: Shared Delivery Framework

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

### Section overline
HOW WE DELIVER

### Heading
One delivery model across every practice

### Subhead
A senior-led, 5-step framework that takes you from discovery to a running system — adoption built in from day one.

### Stepper

1. **Discover** — Assess the current estate, business priorities, and adoption gaps. Define success criteria and target outcomes with stakeholders.
2. **Design** — Architect the solution: data model, platform, governance, security. Design-led wireframes before development.
3. **Build** — Develop on the chosen platform with iterative stakeholder reviews. No big-bang releases.
4. **Deploy** — Roll out to users with workspace setup, security, deployment pipelines, performance tuning, and integration.
5. **Sustain / Optimise** — Drive adoption through training, Centre of Excellence enablement, ongoing governance, monitoring, and continuous improvement.
