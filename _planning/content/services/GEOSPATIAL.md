## SECTION: Use Cases

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Section subheading
Four high-impact geospatial intelligence programs we deliver for enterprises.

### Use-case cards (4 in 2×2)

#### 1. Geospatial Location Intelligence & Market Expansion Analytics
Transform enterprise entity data into a spatial intelligence engine for sales and market growth.
Geo-code and classify 10,000+ enterprise entities from business directories and branch datasets into a centralised spatial master database. Apply proximity mapping, territory optimisation, white-space analysis, and hotspot detection to identify high-potential sales corridors and improve market coverage visibility.
*Industries:* BFSI · Retail · Telecom · Logistics · FMCG.
*Impact:* Improved territory planning · Faster expansion decisions · Optimised market coverage.

#### 2. Geospatial Healthcare Intelligence & Hospital Network Analytics
Analyse healthcare accessibility and optimise facility network expansion using advanced GIS.
Integrate hospital locations, service coverage data, and demographic layers into a spatial healthcare intelligence platform. Use drive-time mapping, KDE, catchment-area modelling, and underserved region (white-space) analysis to optimise healthcare expansion planning and service coverage across target geographies.
*Industries:* Healthcare · HealthTech · Insurance · Public Health · Hospital Networks.
*Impact:* Improved accessibility analysis · Optimised expansion planning · Enhanced coverage visibility.

#### 3. Geospatial Site Suitability & Retail Expansion Analytics
Identify high-potential outlet locations using multi-criteria spatial intelligence.
Integrate outlet data, traffic patterns, competitor presence, POI, and demographic profiles into a spatial suitability engine. Apply multi-criteria analysis, footfall heatmaps, delivery-zone optimisation, and trade-area modelling to evaluate site feasibility and accelerate data-driven outlet expansion decisions.
*Industries:* QSR · Retail · FMCG · Food & Beverage · Franchise Expansion.
*Impact:* Improved site selection · Reduced expansion risk · Optimised delivery coverage.

#### 4. Geospatial Oil Spill Detection & Environmental Monitoring Analytics
Detect, monitor, and respond to environmental risks using remote sensing and GIS.
Process high-resolution aerial and satellite imagery alongside pipeline corridor data to detect oil-spill anomalies, map affected zones, and assess environmental impact. Apply OBIA, spectral analysis, change detection, and geo-fencing to enable proactive risk management and faster emergency response planning across operational corridors.
*Industries:* Oil & Gas · Energy · Environmental Monitoring · Utilities · Pipeline Infrastructure.
*Impact:* Improved spill detection · Faster risk identification · Enhanced compliance readiness.
