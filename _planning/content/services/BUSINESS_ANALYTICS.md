### Section subheading
A proven 5-step delivery framework that moves you from scattered reports to a trusted analytics environment — adoption built in from day one.

### Stepper

| Step | Phase    | What We Do |
|------|----------|------------|
| 01   | Discover | Assess the current BI estate, business priorities, data landscape, and adoption gaps. Define success criteria and target outcomes with stakeholders. |
| 02   | Design   | Architect the BI solution — data model, semantic layer, dashboard structure, governance framework, and security model. Design-led wireframes before development. |
| 03   | Build    | Develop dashboards, semantic models, and governance artefacts on the chosen platform. Iterative delivery with stakeholder reviews. |
| 04   | Deploy   | Roll out to users with workspace setup, row-level security, deployment pipelines, performance tuning, and integration into existing portals or applications. |
| 05   | Sustain  | Drive adoption through training, BI CoE enablement, ongoing dashboard governance, performance monitoring, and continuous improvement cycles. |

## SECTION: Use Cases

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Section subheading
High-impact BI deployments across our priority industries — from executive visibility to operational intelligence.

### Use-case cards (5)

#### Insurance
Enterprise-wide visibility across claims, underwriting, policy servicing, and risk operations. Unified executive dashboards for claims settlement efficiency, underwriting productivity, premium leakage analysis, fraud indicators, policy lapse trends, and agent/channel performance.
*Example dashboards:* Claims TAT · Loss Ratio · Underwriting Productivity · Policy Renewal Trends · Fraud & Risk.
*Outcome:* Improved claims efficiency · Better underwriting control · Stronger portfolio profitability.

#### ITSM & Enterprise Operations
Real-time operational intelligence for enterprise IT and shared services. Centralised BI platforms for SLA adherence, ticket lifecycle, incident trends, service-desk productivity, change-management impact, and infrastructure availability.
*Example dashboards:* SLA Compliance · Incident Trends · MTTR Analysis · Change Success Rate · Service Desk Productivity.
*Outcome:* Higher SLA adherence · Faster issue resolution · Improved operational efficiency.

#### Oil & Gas
Operational visibility across upstream, midstream, and downstream business functions. Advanced analytics dashboards for production monitoring, drilling performance, refinery throughput, asset utilisation, safety compliance, procurement spend, and logistics operations.
*Example dashboards:* Production KPIs · Refinery Performance · Asset Utilisation · HSE Compliance · Procurement & Logistics Analytics.
*Outcome:* Reduced downtime · Improved operational efficiency · Enhanced compliance visibility.

#### Manufacturing
Plant-floor visibility and supply-chain intelligence. Operational dashboards for OEE tracking, production efficiency, downtime root-cause analysis, quality metrics, inventory movement, and supply-chain visibility.
*Example dashboards:* OEE · Downtime Analysis · Quality KPIs · Inventory Visibility · Supply Chain Analytics.
*Outcome:* Higher productivity · Reduced operational waste · Improved delivery reliability.

#### Retail & E-Commerce
Sales and customer intelligence dashboards for revenue trends, customer cohorts, inventory movement, campaign performance, basket analytics, and regional demand forecasting.
*Example dashboards:* Sales Trends · Customer Cohorts · Inventory Turn · Basket Analytics · Campaign Performance.
*Outcome:* Smarter merchandising · Better customer retention · Improved sales visibility.
