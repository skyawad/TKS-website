## SECTION: Problem Statement

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Heading
The Enterprise IT Support Challenge

Tickets pile up. Employees wait. IT teams burn out on the same five requests every day.

Enterprise IT support is stuck in a loop the business can't scale out of. The vast majority of tickets flowing into service desks are repetitive L1 work — password resets, account unlocks, VPN issues, software installs, mailbox access — yet each ticket still consumes a human agent, an hour or more of resolution time, and an employee's entire workflow until it's closed. Service desks scale headcount to keep up. Employees lose hours waiting on routine fixes. And the IT teams who should be enabling transformation spend their best capacity firefighting the same five requests every day. Traditional chatbots and scripted automations promise to fix this — but break the moment a request is ambiguous, requires real action inside enterprise systems, or needs governance the business can defend.

### Common operational pain points
- High volumes of repetitive L1 tickets (password resets, account unlocks, access requests)
- Manual triaging, classification, and routing that delays first-touch response
- Endless clarification loops when employees file incomplete requests
- Slow resolution times — hours or days for routine issues that should take seconds
- Rising service-desk operational cost as ticket volumes grow with the workforce
- Overloaded support teams unable to focus on strategic IT initiatives
- Poor employee experience that erodes trust in IT and slows productivity
- Scripted chatbots that fail on ambiguous requests and can't execute real actions

## SECTION: What TicketIQ Solves

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

### Section subheading
From reactive support → autonomous IT operations. Intake. Classify. Verify. Resolve. Audit.

TicketIQ is an agentic AI ITSM platform that autonomously manages and resolves L1 IT tickets across the enterprise. It captures requests from email, Teams, Slack, and ITSM portals; classifies intent using advanced AI; verifies identity through enterprise-grade controls; executes approved IT actions inside connected systems through APIs and orchestration workflows; escalates intelligently when human judgment is needed; and logs every step into Jira, ServiceNow, and enterprise audit systems. The result: routine L1 work resolves in seconds without a ticket queue, while every action stays governed, traceable, and policy-compliant.

## SECTION: Platform Architecture — How TicketIQ Works

<!-- LOC: UNIVERSAL -->

### Section subheading
A 6-stage agentic decision loop — from employee request to autonomous resolution, with security gates and audit trail built in.

### Stages

| Stage | Phase     | What Happens |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| 01    | Intake    | Requests captured across email, Teams, Slack, ITSM portals, enterprise chat — with channel-native context (identity, location, device) carried into the workflow. |
| 02    | Classify  | AI intent classification interprets the request, resolves ambiguity through conversational follow-ups, and maps to the right ITSM category, action, and risk tier. |
| 03    | Verify    | Security-first governance gates check identity (MFA / OTP), policy rules, and approval requirements based on the request's risk level — before any autonomous action. |
| 04    | Resolve   | Agentic resolution engine executes the approved IT action inside connected enterprise systems through APIs and orchestration workflows. |
| 05    | Escalate  | Low-confidence requests, policy violations, or out-of-scope tickets escalated to L2 with full conversation history, actions performed, and verification status preserved. |
| 06    | Audit     | Every interaction logged into Jira, ServiceNow, and enterprise audit systems — decision history, actions executed, approvals, and SLA tracking captured. |

### Closing line
TicketIQ doesn't just answer IT requests — it runs the IT service desk autonomously, with governance the business can defend.
