## SECTION: Why Our Agents

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

### Heading
Enterprise-grade by default. Audit-ready by design.

### Pillars (4)

1. **Grounded, not hallucinating.** Every response is grounded in your enterprise knowledge — FAQs, SOPs, CRM data, audit trails — through RAG. Generic AI does not get to invent answers.
2. **Safe escalation built in.** Each agent knows what it can answer and exactly when to hand off to a human. Low-confidence, sensitive, or regulated scenarios route to the right person with full context.
3. **Production deployment, not POC theatre.** Cloud · private cloud · hybrid · on-premise — every product ships in the deployment mode regulated buyers in BFSI and government require.
4. **Auditable, observable, governed.** Role-based access, encryption, PII redaction, audit logging, region-specific data residency — every action the agent takes is logged and reviewable.
