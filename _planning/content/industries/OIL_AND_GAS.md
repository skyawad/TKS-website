## SECTION: About the Industry

<!-- LOC: GULF-ADAPT -->

The Oil & Gas sector operates in one of the world's most data-rich yet data-underutilised environments. Sensor streams from refineries, pipeline telemetry, SCADA systems, and logistics networks generate enormous volumes of operational data daily — most of it siloed and underanalysed. In an industry where unplanned downtime costs millions per hour and environmental compliance is non-negotiable, Techknomatic brings full-stack data and AI capabilities purpose-built for upstream, midstream, and downstream operations.
